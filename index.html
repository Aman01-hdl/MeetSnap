Privacy

No bots. No recordings.
No surprises.

While others face lawsuits and IT bans, MeetSnap was built from day one around one principle: your meeting content is yours.

🛡️

The privacy-first meeting tool

Otter.ai faces a class-action lawsuit for allegedly training AI on recordings without user consent. Fireflies and Fathom send bots that visibly join your call — scaring off clients and making sensitive conversations impossible. MeetSnap reads captions only. No audio is ever recorded or transmitted. Nothing is stored on our servers.

🖥️

Processed locally on your device

Captions are captured and processed in your browser. Nothing leaves your machine until you choose to generate.

⚡

Sent to Groq only on generate

The transcript is sent to Groq's API only when you click "Generate Follow-Up" — never before, never automatically.

🚫

Never stored or sold

We never store or sell your meeting content. Once the session ends, it's gone from our side entirely.

🔒

Groq does not train on API data

Groq has confirmed they do not use API data for model training. Your conversations never become someone else's dataset.
